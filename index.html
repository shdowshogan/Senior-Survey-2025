Welcome to the senior survey* website where we present the findings of the senior survey conducted among IITB's graduating batch of 2024. The survey gathers valuable insights from the outgoing batch. The survey is divided into four key sections: Academics, Career, Personal and Miscellaneous. We have also included some comments about their time and experiences here at IITB at the end of the Miscellaneous section.

This website can be best experienced on a personal computer/tablet. Further, in case you're using a mobile phone to view the website, kindly switch to the landscape mode for a better experience. You can hover over/click on the various sections within the pie/bar charts to view the option it's supposed to represent for ease of interpretation. For the questions with responses filtered further on the basis of demographics such as gender or CPI, you can click on the segment buttons to view the chart for that particular demographicÂ segment. We hope you have a fun time going through the survey!

This section explores the academic experiences and achievements of IIT Bombay's graduating seniors. It includes data on course satisfaction, CPI satisfaction, academic interests, attendance importance, and grades received.

This part of the survey delves into the career outcomes and aspirations of graduating seniors. It covers job placements, higher education plans, internships, and factors influencing career choices.
